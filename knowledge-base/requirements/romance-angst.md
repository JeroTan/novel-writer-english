# Romance: Angst Guidelines

## Purpose
To maximize emotional pain, heartbreak, and tension before the ultimate resolution.

## Core Rules
1. **Deep Internal Conflict:** The characters must be their own worst enemies. Their past traumas or deep-seated fears must force them to make heartbreaking decisions.
2. **The Misunderstanding:** If used, it must be rooted in deep character flaws (e.g., assuming the worst because of past betrayal), not just a failure to communicate a simple fact.
3. **Visceral Emotional Pain:** Describe the physical sensation of heartbreak—the hollow chest, the inability to breathe, the numbness.
4. **Earned Catharsis:** The happy ending must be fought for bitterly. The apologies and groveling must match the intensity of the pain inflicted.
