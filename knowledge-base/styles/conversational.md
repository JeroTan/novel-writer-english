# Conversational Style Guidelines

## Objective
To write prose that feels like a natural, intimate conversation with the reader or closely aligns with the protagonist's casual internal monologue.

## Rules
1. **Sentence Structure:** Use shorter, punchier sentences. It's okay to start sentences with conjunctions (And, But, So).
2. **Vocabulary:** Avoid overly flowery or archaic words unless used ironically. Use modern idioms appropriate for the character.
3. **Internal Monologue:** The narrative voice should sound exactly like the POV character thinks. If they curse, the narration curses. If they are sarcastic, the narration is sarcastic.
4. **Show, Don't Tell:** Keep descriptions grounded in immediate physical realities rather than abstract concepts.
