# Literary Style Guidelines

## Objective
To write prose that emphasizes subtext, thematic resonance, and psychological depth.

## Rules
1. **Psychological Depth:** Spend more time in the character's head interpreting the world than merely describing the physical action.
2. **Metaphor & Simile:** Use original, evocative comparisons that reflect the core themes of the story. Avoid clichés.
3. **Ambiguity:** Let characters hold contradicting thoughts simultaneously. Do not over-explain their motivations.
4. **Pacing:** Allow scenes to breathe. It is acceptable to pause the immediate action for a thematic reflection, provided it earns its place.
