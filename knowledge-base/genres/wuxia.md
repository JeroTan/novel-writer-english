# Wuxia (Martial Arts Fiction) Genre Knowledge

## Core Conventions
1. **The Jianghu:** The "Rivers and Lakes"—a martial arts underworld with its own laws, factions, and honor codes, distinct from standard government rule.
2. **Qi and Cultivation:** Martial arts are often powered by internal energy (Qi), allowing for superhuman feats (Qinggong/lightfoot technique).
3. **Honor and Revenge:** Themes heavily focus on filial piety, loyalty to one's sect, and avenging murdered masters or family.
4. **Sects and Clans:** The world is divided into righteous (orthodox) and demonic (unorthodox) sects.

## Key Elements
- **The Wandering Hero:** A protagonist bound by their personal moral code rather than societal law.
- **The Secret Manual:** The pursuit of lost martial arts techniques that grant immense power.
- **Face:** The concept of social standing and reputation is paramount.
