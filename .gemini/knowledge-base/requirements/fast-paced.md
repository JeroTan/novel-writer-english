# Fast-Paced Action Guidelines

## Purpose
To ensure the narrative moves quickly, keeping tension high and the reader engaged.

## Core Rules
1. **Cut the Fat:** Remove unnecessary descriptions of clothing, weather, or mundane travel unless it directly impacts the conflict.
2. **Shorter Paragraphs:** Break up large blocks of text. Action sequences should have rapid-fire paragraphs to simulate speed.
3. **Active Verbs:** Use strong, visceral verbs (slammed, shattered, bolted) instead of passive voice or weak verbs (was running, started to break).
4. **Immediate Consequences:** When a character makes a decision, the result—usually a complication—should happen immediately.
