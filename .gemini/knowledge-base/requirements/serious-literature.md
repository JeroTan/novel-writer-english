# Serious Literature Guidelines

## Purpose
To produce prose that is thematic, introspective, and focused on character psychology and social commentary.

## Core Rules
1. **Subtext over Text:** Never state the theme outright. Let it emerge through character actions and recurring motifs.
2. **Deep Character Study:** Focus heavily on internal monologues, contradicting desires, and moral ambiguity. Characters rarely fit into "good" or "evil" boxes.
3. **Elevated Prose:** Use precise, evocative language. Metaphors should be original and deeply tied to the character's perspective or the setting.
4. **Ambiguity:** Not every plot thread needs a neat bow. Endings can be unresolved or bittersweet if it serves the thematic truth of the story.
