# Romance: Sweet/Fluffy Guidelines

## Purpose
To create a warm, comforting, and low-stress romantic narrative.

## Core Rules
1. **External Conflict:** The conflict keeping them apart should be external (a busy job, a funny misunderstanding, a rival bakery) rather than deep, toxic trauma.
2. **Focus on Intimacy:** Highlight the small, tender moments—shared coffee, gentle touches, inside jokes, and mutual support.
3. **Communication:** The characters should actually talk to each other and resolve minor issues quickly, proving they are a healthy team.
4. **Cozy Atmosphere:** Describe the environment in warm, inviting terms (sunlight, soft fabrics, pleasant smells).
