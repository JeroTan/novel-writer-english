# Minimalist Style Guidelines

## Objective
To write prose that is sparse, direct, and unadorned. (Hemingway style).

## Rules
1. **Adjective/Adverb Diet:** Ruthlessly cut adverbs and unnecessary adjectives. Rely on strong nouns and active verbs.
2. **Short Sentences:** Keep sentences brief and factual.
3. **Iceberg Theory:** State only the surface facts (action and dialogue). The reader must infer the emotional depth and subtext hiding beneath the surface.
4. **Unemotional Narration:** Do not label emotions. State what the character did or said. The action itself must carry the emotional weight.
