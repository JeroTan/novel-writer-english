# Ornate Style Guidelines

## Objective
To write rich, elaborate, and poetic prose. (Tolkien, Rothfuss).

## Rules
1. **Rhythm and Cadence:** Pay close attention to the musicality of the sentences. Use alliteration, assonance, and varying sentence lengths to create a sweeping rhythm.
2. **Elevated Vocabulary:** Use precise, sometimes archaic or highly specific vocabulary. Never use a simple word when a beautiful, precise word will do, provided it doesn't break the flow.
3. **Sensory Immersion:** Describe the environment in lush detail, engaging all five senses. The setting should feel almost mythological.
4. **Epic Tone:** Frame the narrative with a sense of history, scale, and gravitas.
