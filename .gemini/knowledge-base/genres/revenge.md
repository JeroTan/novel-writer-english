# Power Fantasy / Revenge Genre Knowledge

## Core Conventions
1. **The Injustice:** The protagonist is deeply wronged, betrayed, or killed early in the story.
2. **The Return / Rebirth:** They return with new power, knowledge from the future (regression), or a "cheat ability" (system).
3. **Catharsis (Face-Slapping):** The core appeal is the satisfying, systematic dismantling of arrogant antagonists who underestimate the protagonist.
4. **Escalation:** The protagonist must continually face stronger foes, growing in power and influence.

## Pitfalls to Avoid
- **Lack of Struggle:** If the protagonist is too overpowered, the tension vanishes. Give them emotional or strategic challenges their power can't easily solve.
- **Repetitive Villains:** Antagonists must be distinct, not just identical arrogant nobles.
