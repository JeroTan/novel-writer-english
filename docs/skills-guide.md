# Skills Guide

Skills are modular sets of rules and instructions that the AI loads into context when specific tasks are performed.

## Genre Knowledge Skills
Located in `skills/genre-knowledge/`. These provide the AI with deep understanding of genre tropes, pacing expectations, and structures (e.g., Romance, Sci-Fi, Fantasy).

## Quality Assurance Skills
Located in `skills/quality-assurance/`. 
- `pre-write-checklist` is the most important: it forces the AI to load all context before generating prose.
- `consistency-checker` ensures logic remains intact across chapters.

## Writing Technique Skills
Located in `skills/writing-techniques/`. These improve the actual prose, ensuring strong dialogue and scene-sequel structure.
